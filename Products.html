Products

We believe in offering a well-curated selection of financial and non-financial products, tailored to diverse asset classes and risk profiles. Our commitment is to provide holistic, unbiased, and strategic solutions—ensuring seamless access to a dynamic portfolio that grows with your needs.

MUTUAL FUNDS

We offer a full range of mutual fund schemes from every leading Asset Management Company (AMC) in India. Investors can choose between traditional paper form or opt for convenient demat-based investing through our integrated trading account services.

HEALTH INSURANCE

Health insurance ensures comprehensive coverage against medical expenses, safeguarding your financial well‑being. With top plans from India’s best insurers, you gain access to quality healthcare while minimizing out-of-pocket costs.

BONDS

Access a diverse suite of fixed-income investments, including Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs), government-backed bonds, and company fixed deposits—all from top-rated institutions across India.

MARKET LINKED DEBENTURES

Our portfolio includes exclusive Market-Linked Debentures—customized fixed-income instruments tied to the performance of an underlying index or asset. These offer structured returns and may only be available through our platform.

PORTFOLIO MANAGEMENT SERVICES

We offer comprehensive PMS solutions featuring both direct equity and mutual-fund-based strategies curated by India’s top Portfolio Managers. Some strategies are uniquely available only through our network.

LIFE TERM INSURANCE

Secure your family’s future with term insurance. Our portfolio includes the best coverage options with flexible premium, sum assured, and term structures—backed by expert advisory to match your protection goals.

CAPITAL GAIN BONDS

Capital Gain Bonds (under Section 54EC) allow investors to save on long-term capital gains tax by reinvesting in government-backed bonds issued by entities like REC and NHAI. These bonds offer fixed returns and are ideal for conservative, tax-efficient planning.

PERSONAL ACCIDENTAL INSURANCE

Protection that covers accidental death, disability, and related medical costs. Our plans offer financial support if life takes an unexpected turn—ensuring peace of mind for you and your loved ones.
