About Our Company

We are incorporated in the year 2016 and at present have 430+ satisfied clients. We are committed to helping our clients fulfil their financial needs with effective and smart investment assessment. We partnered with NJ Wealth, a B2B platform, to serve our clients. We are certified Mutual Fund and Insurance distributors recognised by AMFI (Association of Mutual Funds of India) and IRDAI (Insurance Regualatory and development authority of India).

We offer comprehensive financial services, having a highly qualified and experienced team of mutual fund distributors. We use our knowledge of insurance, tax laws, and mutual fund investments to suggest options based on long-term and short-term financial needs.
